200 YEARS OF WEDDING FASHION - THE POWERHOUSE MUSEUM

Wedding dress and veil worn by Jennifer Crooks on her marriage to Craig Karpin at The Great Synagogue in Sydney on June 25th 1989.

The wedding dress is an important memento of the wedding day and an opportunity for the bride to 'dress up' in an often expensive and elaborate garment that reflects her personality, cultural background and response to contemporary fashion.
